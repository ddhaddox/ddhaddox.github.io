<h1 id="about" class="about-title">About</h1>

<p class="about-intro">I'm a clinical psychology Ph.D. student at the University of Arizona working in Dr. Zachary Cohen's <a href="https://sites.arizona.edu/personalized-treatment-lab/">Personalized Treatment Lab</a>. My research uses data science methods and intensive longitudinal data to improve how we conceptualize, measure, and predict internalizing psychopathology and its treatment as idiographic and dynamic complex systems.</p>
